Area Attractions

Our Inn is located near many state parks, craft shops, antique malls, exquisite wineries and fine golf courses. Or enjoy the day boating, horseback riding, cross-country skiing or exploring some of our beautiful hiking trails. Within a short distance from the Inn some popular attractions are:

  • John & Annie Glenn Museum
  • Zane Grey Museum
  • National Cambridge Glass Collectors, Inc.
  • Mosser Glass Factory
  • Boyd's Crystal Art & Glass
  • Hopalong Cassidy Museum
  • Living Word Outdoor Drama
  • Pritchard-Laughlin Civic Center
  • Georgetown Vineyards
  • Raven's Glenn Winery
  • The Wild's (North America's Largest Animal Conservation Facility)
  • Roscoe Village
  • Amish Country
  • Whitetail Deer Education Foundation
  • Muskingum College
  • Genealogical Society
  • Terra Cotta Vineyards
  • Christmas in Cambridge, Ohio
