Awards

Honors

Listed on the National Register & Save America's Treasures and built in 1878; Buckeye Bed & Breakfast has been featured in Mid-West Living Magazine with Trevor's tour of the National Rd., RT. 40, Southeastern Ohio Magazine, Discover Ohio, Arrington's Bed & Breakfast Journal, Inn Traveler, 4 years of awards, Mid-West Living (Spring 2006), voted "Best in the USA" & awarded "The Inn with the most Hospitality" in 2003 by inn-goers, FODORS where to weekend around Ohio, the Cincinnati Enquirer, Columbus, Dispatch, Cleveland AND numerous other accolades.

Having earned a TripAdvisor Certificate of Excellence for 5 years in a row, Buckeye Bed & Breakfast is included in TripAdvisor’s Hall of Fame. TripAdvisor is the leading review for inns and hotels to date.

We are very excited to recently be named to bedandbreakfast.com’s list of Top Inns for 2014. With over 450 guest reviews and high marks from a panel of travel writer judges, Buckeye is proud to be in the company of these other fine properties.
